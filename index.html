About

Based in Cape Town (or wherever the seasons take me), I am a front-end web developer and web designer who cares. Cares about the design, about the accessibility and about the people involved. I appreciate clean design, small details and going the extra mile.
I create websites for small businesses, entrepreneurs, and individuals who want to expand their online presence. Who want to get their message out into the world, find the right clients, sell their products or showcase their work.
I have a degree in psychology, a background in basic architectural design, and absolutely love working with people. I'm passionate about kindness, travel and design.

Skills: html5, css3, psd to html, git, bootstrap framework, javascript, and wordpress
